German Shepherd

The German Shepherd (German: Deutscher Schäferhund, German pronunciation: is a breed of medium to large-sized working dog that originated in Germany. The breed's officially recognized name is German Shepherd Dog in the English language (sometimes abbreviated as GSD). The breed was once known as the Alsatian in Britain and Ireland.
